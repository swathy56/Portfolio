About Smart Street Light Monitoring System

The Smart Street Light Monitoring System is designed to optimize energy consumption and ensure efficient street lighting management. The system monitors the status of street lights, detects faults, and adjusts lighting based on environmental conditions to improve energy efficiency and safety.
